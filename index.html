Our Process

Four Steps to a Flawless Finish.

  1. 01

    Consultation

    We assess your vehicle, usage, and goals — then design a protection plan tailored to your standard.

  2. 02

    Decontamination

    Surgical wash, clay, chemical decontamination and paint correction remove every imperfection.

  3. 03

    Precision Install

    Film, coating, and tint applied in a climate-controlled bay by certified installers — zero tolerance for flaws.

  4. 04

    Inspection & Aftercare

    Multi-point quality check under specialty lighting, plus a lifetime workmanship guarantee and aftercare kit.
